Political parties & Candidates

Upcoming elections & Referendums

Local by-elections can take place at any time of the year. Look up our calender for all running by-elections in the different regions of the country. On Thursday 2 May 2019, local government elections are taking palce in 249 local authority areas in the country and for all 11 local councils.

Politico Electoral Commission

We are an independent regulatory agency that was founded in 2011 by the Constitution of our republic. The Commission is responsible for conducting or supervising referendum and elections to any elective body or office established by the Constitution.
